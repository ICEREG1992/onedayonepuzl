about

1d1p, or One Day One Puzzle, is a weekly puzzle "challenge" where players are tasked with creating a unique puzzle in the span of 24 hours (or so). Puzzles are due on Friday night. Puzzles typically come in the form of a single square image with a title, and a small blurb which can be used to include a prompt, puzzle specifications, or a clue sequence.

1d1p is lead by RAZ369 and sus.
